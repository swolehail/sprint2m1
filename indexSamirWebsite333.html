About

Samir Ali (May 19th, 1992 - December 10th, 2022) was a trader, audio engineer, and hip hop artist. His hobbies he grew up playing were baseball, soccer, and basketball. He would find his passion for music and would enjoy creating his own beats. Music was not the only thing he enjoyed in his life, he had a gift working in stock trading, being able to socialize with his friends, networking with various artists and producers, and was highly intellectual. Samir attended Oak Park-River Forest High School, along with his brother, Sohail. Samir would pursue his passion for music shortly after graduating high school, and would collaborate with artists and friends: Supa Bwe, Young Wolf, Blank Oceans etc.
